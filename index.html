FAQ

Common questions

Do parents need to create an account?
No. Parents simply open the booking link, fill in their details, and submit. No account, no password, no app download required. They receive a confirmation email immediately.
Is parent data stored securely?
Yes. All registrations are stored in your school's own private Google Sheet — not on a shared platform. You control who has access. Data never leaves your Google account.
Can we customize the registration questions?
Yes. Each event has its own form builder. Add text fields, dropdowns, or checkboxes in both English and Chinese. Mark fields as required or optional. Every event can have a completely different form.
Does it work with WeChat?
Yes. The booking link is a standard web URL that opens in any browser, including WeChat's built-in browser. You can share it directly in school WeChat groups. No special setup required.
What happens to the data after the event?
It stays in your Google Sheet permanently. You can export it as CSV at any time, archive it, or delete it — you're in full control. We don't retain any copies of your school's data.
