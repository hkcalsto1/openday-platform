Features

Everything your admissions team needs

Built around how HK local and international schools actually run open days — multiple events per year, different audiences, different questions.

📋
Custom Registration Forms
Add your own questions to each event — text fields, dropdowns, required or optional. Every event can have a different form.
🌐
Bilingual — EN / 中文
Parents switch between English and Chinese instantly. All labels, questions, and options display in their preferred language.
✅
Event-Day Check-In
Staff search by name and tap to mark attendance. No paper lists, no spreadsheet hunting. Works on any phone or tablet.
📧
Automatic Confirmations
Every parent receives a bilingual confirmation email with event details the moment they register. No manual sending.
📊
CSV Export
Download the full registrations list for any event — including all custom answers — with one click. Ready for mail merge or reporting.
🔗
Shareable Booking Links
Each event gets a unique URL your team can post on your website, WeChat, or email blast. Parents register in under 2 minutes.
🏫
Manage Multiple Events
Run 10+ events a year? Each gets its own form, its own registrations, and its own booking link. All in one portal.
🔒
School Login, Your Data
Your school has its own login. All your registrations live in a Google Sheet you own. No vendor lock-in, no shared databases.
How it works

Live in a day. Running forever.

We set everything up once. After that, your admissions team handles every event themselves — no IT, no developers, no ongoing fees.

1
We onboard your school
We set up your school account, connect your Google Sheet, and walk your team through the portal. Takes about an hour.
2
You create your event
Log in to the school portal, fill in event details, add your custom questions, and publish. Takes 5 minutes.
3
Share the booking link
Post the link on your website, WeChat, or newsletter. Parents register in their language. Confirmations send automatically.
4
Check in on the day
Staff use the portal to check in families as they arrive. See live attendance counts. Export the full list after the event.
